About BD Plant Care

Common Plant Diseases

Early Blight

Brown circular spots with yellow halo. Common in tomato/potato.

Late Blight

Water-soaked lesions and white fuzzy mold; aggressive rot.

Leaf Spot

Dark circular spots with tan centers; causes leaf drop.

Powdery Mildew

White powdery coating; leaves curl and yellow.

Rust Disease

Orange/yellow pustules on leaf underside.

Mosaic Virus

Light/dark green mosaic pattern; distorted leaves.

Bacterial Blight

Water-soaked spots that turn brown and dry at edges.

Downy Mildew

Yellow patches above; purple/gray fuzz below leaves.
